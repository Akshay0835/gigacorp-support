=== SECTION 1: SHIPPING POLICIES & DELIVERY ===
[Line 6] Q1: Where does GigaCorp ship products?
[Line 7] Answer: GigaCorp ships to all 50 US States, Canada, the United Kingdom, members of the European Union, Australia, Japan, and Singapore. We do not currently ship to India, Russia, or countries in South America due to local import regulations and customs delays.
[Line 8]
[Line 9] Q2: What are the domestic shipping rates and options?
[Line 10] Answer: We offer three domestic shipping speeds within the United States:
[Line 11] - Standard Ground Shipping (3-5 business days): $5.99 (Free for orders over $75).
[Line 12] - Expedited Shipping (2 business days): $14.99.
[Line 13] - Next-Day Delivery (1 business day): $29.99 (Orders must be placed before 1:00 PM EST).
[Line 14]
[Line 15] Q3: Do you offer international shipping?
[Line 16] Answer: Yes, we ship internationally to Canada, the UK, EU countries, Australia, Japan, and Singapore.
[Line 17] - Canada: Flat rate of $19.99, delivery takes 5-9 business days.
[Line 18] - UK & EU: Flat rate of $24.99, delivery takes 7-14 business days.
[Line 19] - Australia, Japan & Singapore: Flat rate of $29.99, delivery takes 10-18 business days.
[Line 20] Please note that international orders may be subject to local import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient and are not collected at checkout.

=== SECTION 2: RETURNS, REFUNDS & REPLACEMENTS ===
[Line 23] Q4: What is GigaCorp's return policy window?
[Line 24] Answer: GigaCorp offers a 30-day return policy for most items. The return window starts from the date of delivery. To qualify for a full refund, items must be in their original condition, unused, and in their original packaging.
[Line 25]
[Line 26] Q5: How do I initiate a return?
[Line 27] Answer: To initiate a return, log into your GigaCorp account, navigate to "Order History," select the item you wish to return, and click "Request Return." Alternatively, you can contact our returns team at returns@gigacorp.com. We will email you a prepaid shipping label and a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) slip.
[Line 28]
[Line 29] Q6: Are there restocking fees or return shipping charges?
[Line 30] Answer:
[Line 31] - For US domestic returns, return shipping is completely free when using our prepaid label. However, a 10% restocking fee applies to electronics and custom assemblies.
[Line 32] - For international returns, the customer is responsible for the cost of return shipping, and no prepaid labels are provided. A flat 15% restocking fee applies to all international returns.
[Line 33]
[Line 34] Q7: How long does it take to receive a refund?
[Line 35] Answer: Once we receive and inspect your returned item at our warehouse, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method. Depending on your financial institution, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the funds to appear on your statement.
